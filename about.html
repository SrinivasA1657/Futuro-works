about Us

I'm SRI VATSHA
(Founder & CEO)

Sri Vatsha the Founder & CEO of Futuro Works graduated with a Bachelor Degree of Fine Arts in the expertise of Photography, Videography, Graphic Designing, Film & Compositing VFX And in various multimedia sectors. He envisioned the significance of the AVGCI sector and started the organisation FUTURO WORKS. He works with the sheer commitment and spirit for the growth of Futuro Works and AVCI sector.

organisation

FUTURO WORKS is an innovative initiative aimed at uplifting the Indian AVGCI (Animation, Visual Graphics, Comics, and Interactive Media) sector by providing education and opportunities to individuals with creative talent. The main objective of FUTURO WORKS is to elevate Indian AVGCI content to global standards. Through its programs, FUTURO WORKS seeks to nurture and develop the skills of aspiring professionals, ensuring they have the tools and knowledge needed to build successful careers in the industry.
